Hello!

I'm currently the Collections and Partnerships Coordinator at the Center for Research Libraries, and an Associate Lecturer at the iSchool at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. In the past, I've worked for the Ivy Plus Libraries Confederation and StoryCorps. I have a Master's degree from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and a Bachelor's degree in English and Creative Writing from the University of Iowa.

I'm an expert web archivist, and am deeply interested in the role collaboration and community-directed collecting plays in acquiring and providing access to born-digital material.
